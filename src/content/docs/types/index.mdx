# Personality Types

Socionics identifies 16 distinct personality types, each with unique characteristics, strengths, and weaknesses. These types are based on how individuals process information and interact with the world.

## The 16 Types

The 16 socionic types are organized into four quadras based on shared values and compatible information processing:

### Alpha Quadra
- **ILE** (Intuitive Logical Extratim) - The Inventor
- **SEI** (Sensory Ethical Intratim) - The Mediator  
- **ESE** (Ethical Sensory Extratim) - The Enthusiast
- **LII** (Logical Intuitive Intratim) - The Analyst

### Beta Quadra
- **SLE** (Sensory Logical Extratim) - The Conqueror
- **IEI** (Intuitive Ethical Intratim) - The Romantic
- **EIE** (Ethical Intuitive Extratim) - The Actor
- **LSI** (Logical Sensory Intratim) - The Inspector

### Gamma Quadra
- **SEE** (Sensory Ethical Extratim) - The Politician
- **ILI** (Intuitive Logical Intratim) - The Critic
- **LIE** (Logical Intuitive Extratim) - The Entrepreneur
- **ESI** (Ethical Sensory Intratim) - The Guardian

### Delta Quadra
- **LSE** (Logical Sensory Extratim) - The Manager
- **EII** (Ethical Intuitive Intratim) - The Humanist
- **IEE** (Intuitive Ethical Extratim) - The Advisor
- **SLI** (Sensory Logical Intratim) - The Craftsman

Each type has its own page with detailed information about their cognitive functions, strengths, weaknesses, and typical behaviors.
