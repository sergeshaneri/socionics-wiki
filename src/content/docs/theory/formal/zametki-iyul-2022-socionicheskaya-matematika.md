 The Moore Correspondence

  

by Steven H. Cullinane on August 20, 2010

here is [a remarkable correspondence](http://finitegeometry.org/sc/24/gentheog2.html) between the 35 partitions of an eight-element set H into two four-element sets and the 35 partitions of the affine 4-space L over GF(2) into four parallel four-point planes. Under this correspondence, two of the H-partitions have a common refinement into 2-sets if and only if the same is true of the corresponding L-partitions (Peter J. Cameron, Parallelisms of Complete Designs, Cambridge U. Press, 1976, [page 60](http://finitegeometry.org/sc/24/Moore_files/110820-Parallelisms60.jpg)). The correspondence underlies the isomorphism* of the group A8 with the projective general linear group PGL(4,2) and [plays an important role](http://finitegeometry.org/sc/24/MOG.html) in the structure of the large Mathieu group M24.
